What Kind Of Pizza Are You?

  1. You got: Bagel Bites

    You are fun with a tongue-in-cheek attitude. People may not take you seriously at times, but once they let you in they’re like, “Why don’t we hang out ALL THE TIME?”

    Via frugalcouponliving.com
  2. You got: Hawaiian Pizza

    Aloha! The epitome of sweet and savory, you are laid back and not afraid to mix it up every now and then. All you need are some good friends and some good vibes in order to have a good time.

    Flickr: stevendepolo / Via Creative Commons
  3. You got: White Pizza

    You are the type of pizza who enjoys flipping the script and taking things in a different direction. In fact, you sort of love flying in the face of conventionality and are not afraid to show it.

    Flickr: nate / Via Creative Commons
  4. You got: All Meat Pizza

    You know what you like and they’re is nothing wrong with that. Also, you know that there is nothing more false than the statement, “Too much of a good thing.” Especially when that thing is meat.

    Flickr: stevendepolo / Via Creative Commons
  5. You got: Chicago-Style Deep Dish

    You are a great pizza friend, like the type who will help you move a couch up three flights of stairs. You also probably have a great laugh and can drink your friends under the table.

    Flickr: maveric2003 / Via Creative Commons
  6. You got: Thin Crust Margherita

    Ooh la la! Oh, you fancy, huh? Fashionable, smart, but not stuck up, you’re the type of pizza that walks into a room and everyone is like, “DAAAAAAAAMN!”

    Flickr: stuart_spivack / Via Creative Commons
  7. You got: Classic New York Slice

    Like the city that never sleeps, you’re a true classic that will never go out of style. Forever hip, you fill the hearts of those around you daily and they couldn’t be happier.

    Flickr: regroupinc / Via Creative Commons
  8. You got: Supreme Pizza

    You got it all going on! A jack of all trades, you get along with everyone because you can relate to them about anything. You’re just a real good person that makes everyone around you that much better.

    Flickr: montage_man / Via Creative Commons
